供应商管理

Supplier Management

良好的供应商关系是提供底价优质产品的最好保证，只有双赢的关系才是最稳固的关系。悦动力致力于与客户和供应商进行良性合作，发展战略伙伴关系，以保证长期的合作共赢。
但是悦动力有严格的供应商准入、审核及评估流程。我们遵守，"Q、C、D、S"原则，对：质量、成本、交付、服务全面评估与管理。最大限度地提高供应商的竞争力，从而保证产品的最佳性价比。

Positive supplier relationship and virtuous cycle is the best guarantee to provide the best quality products. The Win-win relationship is the most stable structure. We are committed to working with our customers and suppliers to develop a strategic partnership to ensure long-term win-win cooperation.
But Utune has fairly strict supplier audit and evaluation process to start cooperation.
"Q, C, D, S" principles: "quality, cost, delivery, service" are the basic principles we comply with to ensure overall assessment and management. So that we can maximize the competitiveness of suppliers, to ensure the best cost performance.
